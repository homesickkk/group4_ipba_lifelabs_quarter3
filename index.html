OUR PROTOTYPE

THEME -- Stop Domestic Violence in Women!

Click the Image for Better View

The poster presents a powerful scene of a massive protest, filled with faceless women standing together against domestic violence. These women come from different professions—nurses, teachers, firefighters, and more—highlighting that domestic violence affects women from all backgrounds.

Each protester holds signs with bold messages: "No Violence," "Break the Silence," "No! Action for #VAWA," and "Stop! Domestic Violence Against Women." Among them, the words "STOP" in "Stop! Domestic Violence" and "NO!" in "No! Action for #VAWA" stand out in striking red, drawing immediate attention to the urgency of the message.

At the top of the poster, a pair of teary eyes symbolize the pain and suffering of victims, reminding viewers of the emotional and physical toll of domestic violence. The background features a crowd of demonstrators raising their fists and holding placards with statements like "Protect Women," "No Means No," and "Violence Doesn't Equal Strength," reinforcing the collective demand for justice, respect, and protection for women.

The use of black and white with selective red elements enhances the seriousness of the issue while emphasizing key points. The faceless figures convey a universal message: this fight is not just for individuals but for all women who have suffered in silence. The poster as a whole calls for immediate action, unity, and awareness in the ongoing struggle to end domestic violence.
